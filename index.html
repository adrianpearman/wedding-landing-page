Our Story

Our story began like many others do these days…online! We were both bravely online dating in the height of the pandemic and managed to find each other before we had to head back indoors for wave 2 lockdown. We got to know each other quickly with a ton of time spent thinking of fun things to do indoors and at some point, through tie dyed sweatshirts, DIY spa days and puzzles we never finished, two unfamiliar people became something way more.
