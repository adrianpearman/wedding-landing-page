Our Story

Our story began like many others do these days…online! We were both bravely dating at the height of the pandemic and managed to find each other before we had to head back indoors for wave 2 of the lockdown. We got to know each other quickly, with a ton of time spent thinking of fun things to do indoors. At some point, through tie dyed sweatshirts, DIY spa days and puzzles we never finished, two unfamiliar people became something much more special. And we can’t wait to celebrate that with the people we love.
